How to schedule COVID-19 Vaccine


you can't choose the COVID-19 vaccine you receive.
It'll be determined by what inventory is availabe
in your area.We'll hold selected time slot(s) and
show your vaccine's manufacturer before you confirm.

One-dose vaccine:

during this session,you'll schedule one appointment
date within a range of available dates.the dose
will be a complete vaccine.

Two-dose vaccine

during this session you'll schedule two separate
appointments.The timings of both equally important
doses is recommended by medical experts.The date
of your second visit will be determined by the date
of your first visit.The second dose is mandatory for
vaccination completion

Info we'll ask for:

The vaccines are no cost to you.We will bill your
insurance provider or the appropriate federal
department.So grab your insurance card(s). If you
don't have insurance,we'll ask you to input a valid driver's
license /state ID number or social security number.
If you dont have this info,it's ok to continue
scheduling your vaccine.
